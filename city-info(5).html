Hay Mohammadi

Hay Mohammadi is a prominent urban development located in the northeast of Agadir, Morocco. Initiated in 2002 to address the city's housing deficit, this planned community spans approximately 465 hectares and was designed to accommodate around 100,000 residents. Today, it stands as a model of modern urban planning, continuing to evolve and expand with new amenities and infrastructure.
