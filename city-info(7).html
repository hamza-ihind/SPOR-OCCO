Hay Salam

Hay Salam is a vibrant residential neighborhood situated in the southern part of Agadir, Morocco. Known for its family-friendly environment, it offers a perfect blend of modern amenities and traditional Moroccan charm, making it an ideal place for both residents and visitors seeking authentic local experiences.
