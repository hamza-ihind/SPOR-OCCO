Bensergao District

Bensergao is a dynamic district located on the outskirts of Agadir, Morocco where traditional Berber heritage meets modern living. This vibrant area offers both residents and visitors a rich tapestry of cultural experiences, recreational activities, and natural attractions, all while maintaining its authentic Amazigh character.
