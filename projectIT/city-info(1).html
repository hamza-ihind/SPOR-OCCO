Talborjt

Description:

Talborjt (تلبورجت) is one of the most iconic and culturally rich neighborhoods in Agadir, Morocco. Once the historic heart of the city before the devastating 1960 earthquake, Talborjt was completely rebuilt and now stands as a symbol of Agadir’s resilience and modern rebirth.

Activities:

  • Local Shopping
  • Café Culture
  • Live Music & Events
  • Visit Mohamed V Mosque
  • Relax in Public Gardens
