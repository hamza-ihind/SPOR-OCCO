Founty

Description:

Founty (فونتي) is one of the most modern and upscale neighborhoods in Agadir, Morocco, located just a short walk from the city’s famous beach and coastline. Known for its quiet streets, luxury accommodations, and beachside ambiance, Founty is often the go-to area for tourists and upper-class residents alike.

Activities:

  • Resort Stays
  • Beach Activities
  • Dine by the Sea
  • Sunset Views
  • Wellness and Spa
