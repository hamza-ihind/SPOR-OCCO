Founty District

Founty (فونتي) is one of the most modern and upscale neighborhoods in Agadir, Morocco, located just a short walk from the city's famous beach and coastline. Known for its quiet streets, luxury accommodations, and beachside ambiance, Founty is often the go-to area for discerning tourists and upper-class residents alike. This exclusive enclave offers a perfect blend of Mediterranean sophistication and Moroccan hospitality.
