Dakhla

Description:

Dakhla (الداخلة) is one of the largest and most dynamic residential neighborhoods in Agadir, Morocco. It is known for its affordable housing, active community life, and practical amenities, making it a favorite among families, students, and working-class residents.

Activities:

  • Business and Professional Services
  • Cafés and Social Life
  • Affordable Local Eateries
  • Proximity to Universities
  • Walking and Socializing
